Mozarella Parma Sandwich

Description

A great, tasty and quick sandwich to make, for lunch or as a snack. Can be made in tiny pieces as a bite to serve at parties.

Ingredients

  • Ciabatta bread, one piece (or your favorite other bread - your pick!)
  • Butter
  • 0.75 of a mozarella, sliced thin
  • 3 slices of parma ham
  • A few basil leaves
  • 1/3rd of a tomato, thinly sliced
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Carve up your bread into two slices
  2. Butter it well
  3. Add lots of thin slices of mozarella, to entirely cover the bread
  4. Add a tiny amount of salt and pepper
  5. Add the parma ham slices on top
  6. Add the tomato slices on top
  7. Add another round of salt and pepper on the tomatoes
  8. Finish off with some basil leaves. The more, the better, really.
