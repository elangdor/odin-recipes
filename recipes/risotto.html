Risotto

Description

A tasty italian dish. The base is easy to get right, and you can make all kinds of variations you can think of!

Ingredients

  • 400g Arborio rice
  • 4 shallots, finely diced
  • Butter
  • Olive oil
  • Half a bottle of white wine
  • Another half bottle of white wine
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1l Chicken stock
  • 100g of pecorino cheese, shredded
  • Salt, pepper

Steps to reproduce

  1. Start by mincing the shallots
  2. Start warming the chicken stock in a pot
  3. Put the stove on medium heat
  4. In a frying pan with high edges, saute√© the shallots in olive oil and butter
  5. When the shallots are golden, pour in the rice
  6. Stir the rice for a few minutes, they should turn transparent
  7. Pour in wine so the contents of the pan are covered
  8. Stir the rice gently
  9. Pour a glass of wine for the chef
  10. When the wine has vaporized, pour some chicken stock to almost cover the rice
  11. Keep stiring the risotto from time to time, and add more stock when the previous has vaporized
  12. After about 20 minutes, the Risotto should have the correct consitency
  13. When its done, take the pan from the heat, add a fat slice of butter and the pecorino, stir and.
  14. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  15. Serve
